About Curry

Early Life and Background

Full Name: Wardell Stephen Curry II

Born: March 14, 1988, in Akron, Ohio, USA

Height: 6'2" (1.88 m)

Weight: 185 lbs (84 kg)

College: Davidson College (2006–2009)

Drafted: 7th overall by the Golden State Warriors in the 2009 NBA Draft.

NBA Career

Team: Golden State Warriors (2009–present)

Position: Point Guard

Jersey Number: 30

Stephen Curry's Achievements

    NBA Championships: 4-time NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)

    NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP): 2-time MVP (2015, 2016)

    NBA All-Star Selections: Multiple-time All-Star

    3-Point Records: All-time leader in three-pointers made

    NBA Scoring Titles: Multiple-time scoring champion

    Golden State Warriors Records: Franchise leader in wins and playoffs

    Curry is known for his quick release and deep three-point range. He has revolutionized the game with his shooting and playmaking ability.

    Curry’s success has influenced the NBA, with teams focusing more on three-point shooting and “small ball” strategies.

    Curry is involved in numerous philanthropic efforts and media projects, including supporting youth development and producing content like his documentary series.

    Curry’s legacy is secure as one of the best players in NBA history, impacting the game both on and off the court.
